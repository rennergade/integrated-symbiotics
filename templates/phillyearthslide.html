Philly Earth

Integrated Symbiotics partnered with Philly Earth, a permaculture education center which is a sub-division of the Village of Arts and Humanities, to build an off-grid solar powered aquaponics system in their earthship style greenhouse. The aquaponics system is made from recycled blue 55 gallon barrels and donated materials. The main fish tank holds 300 gallons and houses goldfish and tilapia, there is also a 100 gallon sump tank. The system is powered by 4 - 230W solar panels which feed to a battery storage system. This system acts as an educational tool at PhillyEarth, teaching students that normally would not be exposed to such subjects about sustainability, agriculture, renewable energy, and biology.
